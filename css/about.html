ABOUT US

Foodies Restuarant


Foodies Restaurant, established in 2015 was launched with a single vision; to serve the people of Ahmedabad with real Punjabi food. It is the preservation of the high standards of Indian cuisine and the consistent delivery of this service for over 33 years that makes Foodies Restaurant unique. Foodies Restaurant ensures that its diners are transported to the mainlands of Punjab through every bite of their meal.
